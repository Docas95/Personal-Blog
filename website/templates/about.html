Who am I?

You can call me Docas. At the time of the creation of this blog I am a 16 year old highschooler and I'll be starting my last year of school in a few weeks.

In terms of personality, I am quite a shy and introverted person. Being with people isn't really my thing and I'm not very good at socializing with others, I am the most comfortable when I'm alone or with a small group of close friends. I sometimes find it nice to meet new people, but it's usually a stressing situation. I've been told I have a great sense of humor, I tend to make people laugh with my jokes, wich is something I enjoy a lot, although I wouldn't call myself a comedian or anything like that. I'm pretty creative and love to come up with new ideas about various topics and find new ways to see the world.

When it comes to my interests, I tend to enjoy anything that envolves creativity and problem solving. I love drawing and spend way more time than I would like to admit playing videogames. I also have a great interest in technology, especially cybersecurity. I started learning how to program a few months ago using Harvard's cs50 and I feel like I've finally reached a level where I can call myself a programmer. I'm not super good at coding, but it's something I love doing and, as of right now, I think I'm finally able to use my skills to solve basic problems using code and I can also write basic programs, like simple websites and videogames, with a little help from google. The area of technology that interest me the most is cybersecurity. Ever since I was a kid, I've always enjoyed the idea of being a hacker that is smart enough to hack into any system. As I got older I decided to learn more about the topic, I main used youtube youtubes and spotify podcasts as my teachers and I learned a lot from them. I think cybersecurity is a super important field, I mean in the age of technology what can be more important than making sure that computers and all the data they store are protected at all times? I also really enjoy that it's a field that requires a lot of problem solving skills and constant learning. I'm still young and have a lot to learn, but I can't wait to be able to contribute to this field when I'm older.


What is this blog?

I have always enjoyed technology and a few months ago I decided to take my interest more seriously and decided to learn to code. While doing that I also started listening to tech related podcasts, most of them related to cybersecurity, as that has always been the area of technology that interests me the most.

While I think Spotify and Youtube have taught me a lot about the basics of cybersecurity, I would like to deepen my knowledge in it. I created this blog as a way to document everything I learn about related to cybsersec. Besides it being away to keep things organized, I think it'll also serve as motivation to study, since I won't want to leave this blog without any updates for too long.
